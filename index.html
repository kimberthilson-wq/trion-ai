System Philosophy

Constrained Intelligence

TRION was designed around constrained intelligence rather than unrestricted AI autonomy. Every decision passes through multiple validation gates before reaching paper execution.

//

Deterministic Validation

Every AI output is validated against deterministic rules before execution. No ambiguity in the decision chain.

!!

Independent Risk Authority

Risk engine operates independently of AI reasoning. When risk says no, the system stops. No exceptions.

{}

Constrained AI Outputs

AI generates structured, typed outputs within strict boundaries. No freeform responses in critical paths.

>_

Observability First

Every decision, every signal, every state change is logged and traceable. Full transparency by default.

[]

Runtime Traceability

Complete audit trail from market tick to paper order. Replay any decision with full context.

X

Fail-Closed Execution

Any failure in the pipeline halts execution. The system never proceeds with incomplete validation.
